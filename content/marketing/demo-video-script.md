# Content Forge — Demo Video Script

**Target length:** 45–60 seconds  
**Format:** Screen recording with voice-over + light background music  
**Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (YouTube) or 9:16 (Shorts/Reels/TikTok)

---

## Scene 1: Hook (0:00–0:08)

**Visual:** Dark-themed dashboard, camera slowly zooms in.

**On-screen text:** “What if AI wrote your blog posts end-to-end?”

**Voice-over:**  
“What if you could drop in a topic and get a researched, SEO-optimized blog post with a cover image — automatically?”

---

## Scene 2: Create a job (0:08–0:20)

**Visual:** Click “New Job,” type topic “The future of serverless AI workflows,” select tone “Professional,” length “Medium,” click “Run.”

**On-screen text:** “Topic → Tone → Go”

**Voice-over:**  
“Enter a topic, pick your tone and length, and hit run. Content Forge handles the rest.”

---

## Scene 3: Pipeline in motion (0:20–0:35)

**Visual:** Job detail page. Progress bar moves through Research → Outline → Draft → SEO → Image → Notify. Each stage completes in real time.

**On-screen text:** “Research → Outline → Draft → SEO → Image → Notify”

**Voice-over:**  
“It researches, outlines, writes the draft, optimizes for SEO, generates a cover image, and emails you when it’s done.”

---

## Scene 4: Result + exports (0:35–0:48)

**Visual:** Scroll through finished draft. Click “Export” and select PDF, then PowerPoint.

**On-screen text:** “Markdown • HTML • JSON • PDF • PowerPoint”

**Voice-over:**  
“Export as Markdown, HTML, JSON, PDF, or PowerPoint. Or schedule recurring posts so your content never stops.”

---

## Scene 5: CTA (0:48–0:60)

**Visual:** GitHub repo page and live app URL side by side.

**On-screen text:** “Open-source on GitHub” / “Try the live demo”

**Voice-over:**  
“Content Forge is open-source. Star it on GitHub and try the live demo today.”

---

## Production notes

- Record in dark mode for visual consistency.
- Use smooth cursor movements and quick cuts between stages.
- Keep text overlays large and readable on mobile.
- Add subtle whoosh sound effects when stages complete.
- End with a 2-second pause on the GitHub CTA.
